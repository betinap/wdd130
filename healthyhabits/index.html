Healthy Food

I will share some topics that will help me improve my health.
I learned that a person has a perfect body like a machine. For this reason, it needs fuel from food to move, whereas exercise is a great way to permit us to be strong and agile.
I will include everything I ingest in the food category, like foodstuff and natural medicine, because these will metabolize and generate an effect on our anatomy. If I put good quality fuel in our car, it will have a great rate; my body works the same. It needs good quality food. When I ingest a lot of ultra-processing food and medicine, my system has a lot of work to process and generate several quantities of waste. And this system works a lot without need.
So I understand that I need to ingest high-quality food, not necessarily expensive, so that my organism will be efficient for a long time.
If you want to know how I changed my diet and its effect on my life, go to the Food page.

Healthy Exercise

Regarding exercise, the benefits of physical activity are many and well-known. I will summarize them in immediate benefits such as:
*Managing your weight.
*Reducing risks to your health.
*Strengthening your bones and muscles.
*Improving your ability to do daily activities and prevent falls.
*Increasing your chances of living a longer life.
